Display Pricelist Price On Products

With the help of this module you can display the price of the products according to the pricelists in Product Form. Also it allows you to hide the pricelists price from the product.

Features
  • Admin can check the product prices on the basis of pricelist in product form
  • Admin can also hide the pricelist price on the product
  • No Backend Configuration, just install and use
